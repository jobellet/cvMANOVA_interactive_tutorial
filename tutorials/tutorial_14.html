Non-Spatial Maintenance

Decoding Sensitivity

While univariate methods failed to find contiguous activation blobs, cvMANOVA revealed a distributed, fine-grained pattern across 18 cortical regions.

Univariate Blob Pattern Distinctness (D)
Why near zero? Overall metabolic demand is identical because the physical sounds presented are exactly the same. Maintenance-related activations are distributed, preventing univariate methods from detecting contiguous blobs.
