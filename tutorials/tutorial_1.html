Key Concept

1

ANOVA

Univariate. Tests one voxel at a time. Misses distributed patterns.

2

MANOVA

Multivariate. Models pattern geometry but biased in high-p/low-df settings.

3

cvMANOVA

Cross-validated. Unbiased estimator. Null distribution centered at 0.
