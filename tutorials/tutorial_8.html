Concept Overview

Standard Machine Learning approaches like Support Vector Machines (SVMs) often ignore the specific correlation structure of the noise between different voxels. cvMANOVA explicitly incorporates this through the noise covariance matrix.

In this interactive tutorial, you will be able to adjust the noise variance and spatial correlation to see exactly when standard Euclidean distance breaks down and produces false positives.
